Tennese? Who's that?

Misunderstood, and as a result, full of surprises.

Tennese's are often observed at face value, it's such a shame until you realize they also .. look at others at face value (Very hypocritical). They are also perceived as aggressive, or angry at times. As aggressive as a chihuahua you could say, RBF-looking ass. But when you dive in, they're not not all of these things lol, though they are still, very human. So much expectation, judgment, and perception from others weigh them down. Makes them spend all day in bed, unmotivated, unwilling to face the day and the people that want things from them. Expect things from them. Tennese's just wanna vibe, be happy, feel free, and experience new things. Learn, because they acknowledge they don't know many things (very self-aware/conscious) and this ability to still power through their day, despite all the setbacks, make's Tennese's very admirable. Respected, for one reason or another (sometimes fear). All physical features aside, though they are often very attractive. Tennese's are honest, brutally so. Caring, loving, self-sacrificing even. And, if you ever have the chance to witness a more vulnerable side to a Tennese, treasure them.
